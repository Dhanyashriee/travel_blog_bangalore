Iconic Establishments

Mavalli Tiffin Room (MTR)

Lalbagh Road

Established in 1924, MTR is legendary for its authentic South Indian breakfast and lunch. Famous for their Rava Idli, which was invented here during World War II rice shortages.

Must Try:
  • Masala Dosa
  • Rava Idli
  • Filter Coffee
  • Bisibelebath
6:30 AM - 11 PM ₹300 for two

Vidyarthi Bhavan

Gandhi Bazaar

A heritage restaurant serving the city since 1943. Known for their unique style of serving crispy masala dosas and their vintage ambiance.

Must Try:
  • Butter Masala Dosa
  • Kesari Bath
  • Filter Coffee
6:30 AM - 11:30 AM, 2:30 PM - 8 PM ₹150 for two

Central Tiffin Room (CTR)

Malleshwaram

This iconic establishment has been serving some of the city's best benne (butter) dosas since 1920. A favorite among locals and tourists alike.

Must Try:
  • Benne Masala Dosa
  • Khara Bath
  • Coffee
7:30 AM - 12:30 PM, 4 PM - 8 PM ₹200 for two

Popular Street Food Destinations

VV Puram Food Street

Famous for its variety of South Indian street food and snacks. Best visited in the evening for the full experience.

Popular Items:
  • Paddu
  • Holige
  • Gulkand Ice Cream

Best Time: 6 PM - 11 PM

Johnson Market

A paradise for meat lovers, known for its kebabs and rolls. One of the oldest food streets in Bangalore.

Popular Items:
  • Seekh Kebabs
  • Shawarma
  • Grilled Chicken

Best Time: 5 PM - 10 PM
